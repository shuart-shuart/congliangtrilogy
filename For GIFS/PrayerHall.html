仨兰

Many Chinese were killed during the Japanese Occupation due to the Sook Ching Operation and the Kempeitai's massacre of anyone allegedly anti-Japanese. The mass killings were horrifying and Ah Jiao greatly empathized with those who sacrificed, as it was impossible to pick up the corpses, Ah Jiao set up an obscured prayer hall instead. The site of prayers and resting place was at Mount Faber, which was close to Sentosa, one of the larger killing sites. Concurrently, the prayer hall served as a point of information dispersal during this period of White Terror. Other than Shuang Shuang's help, Ah Jiao was aided by Ah Geok. Ah Geok and many other Samsui Women served on the Dalforce, and they were angered and afflicted by the Japanese purging their commanderies. Ah Geok continued to run the prayer hall continued even after the other two lady's capture.
